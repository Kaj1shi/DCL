About Drescher Consult Limited

A Ugandan ICT products and services provider focused on reliable solutions, reduced downtime, and customer satisfaction.

Background

One of Uganda's leading providers of ICT products and services.

Drescher Consult Ltd has been involved in the provision of IT solutions for the last 14 years, continuously transforming itself to meet the changing business environment of its customers.

DCL serves government, NGOs, public and private companies, schools, hospitals, financial service organizations, and individual consumers with solutions tailored to client requirements.

Core competencies

ICT capability.

  1. InfrastructureInfrastructure, storage, messaging, networking, telephone, desktop, and laptop solutions.
  2. ProductsLaptops, servers, desktops, printers, scanners, UPSs, peripherals, accessories, and networking equipment.
  3. SupportWorkshop repairs, onsite recovery and repairs, hardware and software maintenance, and managed support services.
  4. ConsultingSystems integration, IT system audits, consultancy, installation services, and ITIL-aligned service management.

Vision

To be at the forefront of the market with special attention to complete customer satisfaction, reducing downtime, and supplying the right equipment for everyday use.

Quality

DCL is committed to quality in all respects of the business and to delivering information technology based solutions that meet customer expectations.

Service
